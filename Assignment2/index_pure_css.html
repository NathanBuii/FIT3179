The Japanese Domestic Market (JDM) Car Scene in USA, Seen Through Craigslist

With the massive impact of The Fast and Furious and Initial D franchises in the 90s, the tuner scene in the USA exploded in popularity. Legendary cars like the Nissan Skyline, Toyota Supra and Mazda RX-7 are coveted for their aesthetic and build potential around the world. This visualisation will explore the JDM car market within the USA as shown through over 200,000 Craigslist used car listings (pre September 2020).
